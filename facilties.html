Our Facilities

The Farm Activity Centre offers a range of exciting facilities for visitors to enjoy. The onsite shop features a selection of local plants and fresh produce, all grown right on the farm, allowing guests to take home a taste of the countryside. The small petting zoo is home to farm animals, providing a hands-on experience for children and families to interact with goats, sheep, and more. The cafe serves hot, refreshing drinks, perfect for warming up after a day of exploring. There's also a play park for children to enjoy, as well as scenic walks for families to enjoy the natural beauty of the farm together. It’s a perfect day out for all ages!

Our Petting Zoo

The petting zoo at the Farm Activity Centre is a delightful experience for visitors of all ages. It features a variety of friendly farm animals, including goats, sheep, rabbits, and chickens, all of which are lovingly cared for on the farm. Children and families can interact with the animals, feeding them and learning about their daily lives. The hands-on experience provides a wonderful opportunity to connect with nature and the farm’s residents in a safe and enjoyable environment. Whether you're petting a soft lamb or watching chickens peck around, the petting zoo offers a fun and educational experience for everyone.

Local Shoppe

The shop offers a wonderful selection of local produce, all sourced directly from the farm and surrounding areas of Melrose, Scotland. Visitors can find fresh, seasonal fruits and vegetables, homemade jams, and artisanal products that showcase the region's rich agricultural heritage. In addition to the farm's own harvest, the shop features a variety of locally grown plants, perfect for gardening enthusiasts. Every item reflects the quality and craftsmanship of Melrose, allowing guests to take home a true taste of the Scottish Borders. Whether you're shopping for fresh ingredients or unique souvenirs, the shop is a celebration of local farming and community.

Our Cafe

The café offers a warm and inviting space to relax and enjoy a selection of hot, refreshing drinks. Whether you're in the mood for a steaming cup of coffee, a soothing herbal tea, or a rich hot chocolate, there’s something to satisfy every taste. The cozy atmosphere makes it the perfect spot to unwind after a walk or visit to the petting zoo. With views of the surrounding countryside, it’s a wonderful place to enjoy a moment of calm, savoring your drink while taking in the beautiful farm setting. The café is a welcoming retreat for families, friends, and visitors to enjoy a comforting break.

The Play park

The play park is a fun-filled space designed to keep children entertained and active. With a variety of play equipment, including swings, slides, and climbing frames, there’s something for kids of all ages to enjoy. The park is set in a safe, spacious area, allowing children to explore, play, and interact with others in a beautiful outdoor environment. It’s the perfect spot for families to relax while the little ones burn off some energy, all while surrounded by the peaceful countryside. Whether it’s a sunny afternoon or a breezy day, the play park offers endless fun for kids and peace of mind for parents.

The Walks

The walks at the Farm Activity Centre offer visitors a chance to explore the stunning countryside of Melrose at their own pace. These scenic trails wind through rolling hills, lush meadows, and quiet woodlands, providing breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes. The walks are perfect for families, with easy-to-follow paths that are suitable for all ages and abilities. Along the way, visitors can enjoy the natural beauty of the area, spot local wildlife, and take in the peaceful atmosphere. Whether you're looking for a leisurely stroll or a more invigorating hike, these walks offer a wonderful opportunity to connect with nature and experience the charm of the Scottish Borders.
