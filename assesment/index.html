"At Melrose Farm House, You'll experience a vacation like no other".

Welcome to The Melrose Farm Adventure Centre, nestled just outside the charming town of Melrose, Scotland. Once a working farm, we've transformed this beautiful space into a vibrant family activity centre, where fun and discovery await around every corner. Whether you're looking to explore our wide-open spaces, engage in hands-on farm experiences, or enjoy exciting outdoor adventures, our newly renovated facilities offer something for everyone. With a warm, welcoming atmosphere and a touch of Scottish countryside charm, it's the perfect place for family outings, group activities, or a day of exploration. Join us for unforgettable memories in a truly special setting!

Our facilities

Melrose Farm House is nestled just outside Melrose, Scotland, this charming farm House offers a delightful range of facilities for guests to enjoy. Visitors can browse a small shop stocked with local plants and produce, perfect for taking home a piece of the countryside. Families will appreciate the petting zoo, where children can interact with friendly animals, while the café provides a cozy spot to relax with homemade treats. There's also a play park for little ones to enjoy, as well as scenic walking trails that invite guests to explore the stunning surrounding landscape.

The Local Area

in the tranquil Scottish Borders, just outside the charming town of Melrose, the small farm house offers guests a peaceful escape surrounded by rolling hills, lush farmland, and the gentle flow of the River Tweed. The area is rich in natural beauty, perfect for outdoor enthusiasts who enjoy walking, cycling, and birdwatching. Melrose itself is a picturesque town known for its historic Abbey, quaint shops, and cozy cafes. Visitors can also explore nearby trails and heritage sites, including the renowned Eildon Hills, all while experiencing the warmth and hospitality of this idyllic rural retreat.
